Website

This is an unofficial website for distributing pre-built binaries of GNU/IceCat for Linux, Windows and MacOS. It is not officially endorsed by GNU, but they were informed of it.

Why

It is not possible to completely remove telemetry from firefox by changing preferences, because telemetry is built-in or hardcoded in the binary installer and executables provided by Mozilla, including the ones that can be downloaded from FTP.

The ONLY way around is recompiling the source code after removing or altering some parts. This is the main reason why Icecat exists.

Differences

IceCat removes all proprietary blobs included in IceCat, as well as telemtry. It is also bundled with additional add-ons, such as JShelter, LibreJS and Privacy Redirect.

We do not collect data, or log your activities on this site, not even to improve it or improve "your experience".
